Core Class Features

Siphon Element

  • As an action, you can attempt to draw elemental energy from a significant source of an element (e.g., a bonfire for Fire, a stream for Water, a strong gust for Air, rich soil/large rock for Earth) within 30 feet.
  • You gain EP of the corresponding type. The DM determines the availability and potency of elemental sources. A particularly potent or large source might grant more points or allow siphoning for multiple turns.
  • When you siphon an element, the source is visibly diminished or temporarily exhausted (e.g., a patch of grass withers, a small fire dims, water stills, a breeze fades). When draining larger quantities, bonfires may turn to ash, rivers may run dry, forests may wither and the air might become stale and toxic.

Elemental Points

  • You start with 0 EP and they are lost after a long rest.

Spell Slot Recovery

  • You regain 4 levels worth of spell slots during every short rest (how you divide these levels is up to you). This feature can be used up to twice per day.

Luna's Urging

  • Luna's presence becomes more distinct. You might hear her whispers, feel her emotions, or receive visions, especially when siphoning or overloaded. This can be a source of plot hooks or guidance (or misdirection!).
  • Mechanically, once per long rest, when you fail a saving throw, you can choose to succeed instead as Luna's power surges through you. If you do so, you immediately gain 1d4 EP of a random type (roll a d4). 1 → Fire. 2 → Water. 3 → Earth. 4 → Air.
